Courier is a revolutionary messaging app that combines the convenience of digital communication with
the personal touch of traditional mail. Inspired by the reliability and intimacy of handwritten letters,
Courier allows users to send not only digital messages, but also physical
letters to their loved ones.

MESSAGE PRIVATELY

Send with confidence, just like a traditional letter,
knowing that your personal messages will
remain private and reach their intended recipient,
whether they're tech-savvy or prefer
a more traditional approach to staying in touch.

Whether it's your confessions, your difficult debates,
or your silly inside jokes,
your conversations need to be protected.

PRIVACY

Your privacy is our priority. With end-to-end encryption,
you can be sure that your personal messages stay
between you and who you send them to.
In addition to this, the letter you send
will also be securely sealed and received by your recipient
through our layers and layers of security
and protection.

At Courier,
we take the security and integrity of your messages seriously. When you send a letter through our platform, it is handled with care and confidentiality.
Our system ensures that your letter is printed and packaged in a secure facility,and then shipped via our very own trusted postal carrier.
Each letter is assigned a unique tracking number, allowing you to monitor its progress from the moment it's sent to the moment it's
delivered. Our postal carriers use secure and reliable logistics networks, with features such as signature upon delivery and
GPS tracking, to ensure that your letter reaches its intended recipient safely and efficiently. Additionally, our platform
is designed with multiple layers of encryption and access controls, protecting your personal data and the content of your
letters from unauthorized access. With Courier, you can trust that your letters will be delivered with the utmost
care and security, bringing you closer to your loved ones who matter most.
